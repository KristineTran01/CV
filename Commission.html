Terms of Service

General

  • I am comfortable with most themes, but certain sensitive themes such as illegal themes, abuse, drugs and others that personally make me uncomfortable will be refused.
  • I keep all the rights to my art. I maintain the right to post in on my portfolio and social media pages. If for any reason you would prefer your commission remain private, please let me know in advance
  • If you are uncomfortable with me potentially streaming or recording the process of creating your commission, please let me know in advance
  • I maintain the right to refuse any commission without further explanation
  • I fully understand how hectic life can be and I am completely okay with commissioners taking a while to get back to me during our communication, but ghosting for months will result in blacklisting.
  • In the rare case of a commissioner failing to pay, I will repurpose the commissioned artwork for profit to make up for it.
  • For commissions depicting existing, real people, I am much stricter on what I am comfortable to draw, and will err on the side of caution.
  • You will recieve your final, full-size files as .pngs, through email or google drive link (deviantart customers can also get it through deviantart sta.sh). Feel free to ask for different file types though.
  • The more clear a customer is in what they are looking for, the smoother and faster the commission will go, and the better the chances the result will be closer to what you're looking for. I do not mind vagueness at all, especially with custom designs, but I need to put this as a note, because I sincerely want you to be happy with the end result.

Payment

  • I accept payment only through paypal invoice, and in case the customer prefers it for any reason, through Ko-fi.
  • My prices are in USD, but I do also accept the euro equivalent
  • For anything over $70 I ask for half the payment upfront. For less than that, I ask for the payment at the completion of the commission and before sending the final full-size files.
  • Once the commission has progressed past the rough sketch stage, I do not do refunds.

Communication

  • Communication throughout the process of the commission can be done via email, discord, twitter and instagram DMs as well as deviantart notes. Be aware that instagram DMs are unreliable in their notifications so they are the least recommended of the list, but still a valid option for those that prefer them.

Timing & Process

  • Most simple commissions take anywhere from 2 weeks to a month and a half to complete.
  • I try to give updates at least once a week on the progress of all commissions to each customer individually. Only in the case of rare emergencies will this be affected.
  • If for any reason you wish for your commission to be done in a specific time frame, this needs to be discussed beforehand and there might be a rush fee.
  • I check in with customers as much as I can throughout the process to ensure that everyone is happy with the end result. I usually send updates and the commissioner can ask for corrections and changes in the: rough sketch, clean lines and color phases.
  • For the rough sketch phase, a reasonable amount of sketches from scratch can be offered.
  • In regards specifically to custom designs, it's my top priority that the design fits the commissioner's needs, so I can happily offer a larger amount or test rough sketches and variations even further down the line if needed.

Use of My Art

  • The art I create is for personal use of the individual commissioner only. For professional use (including use in streams, monetized videos, merch shops etc) or businesses the prices and process will differ and can be discussed seperately from anything mentioned on this website via the means you can find on the "contact" page
  • I do not allow my art to be used in NFTs or anything that has to do with NFTs , crypto etc.
